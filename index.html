About the Workshop

Background

Quantum technologies are revolutionizing secure communication, computation, and simulation. Effective characterization and verification are crucial as systems scale.

Objectives

Bring together experts to address scalability challenges and develop efficient QCVV methods for large quantum systems.

Hybrid Format

Combining physical sessions in Shanghai with virtual participation options, featuring interactive workshops and live-streamed talks.

The rapid advancement of quantum technologies brings both opportunities and challenges in system characterization. Traditional tomographic methods become impractical as quantum systems scale, creating an urgent need for innovative QCVV approaches. This workshop will:

  • Showcase latest developments in efficient characterization protocols
  • Explore hybrid verification methods combining physical and theoretical approaches
  • Facilitate collaboration between academia and industry
